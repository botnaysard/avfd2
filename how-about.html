How It Works

Getting a Very Fairy Door is really special!

Here are some tips from your very fairy friends to help you get ready for them to visit:

  1. A wall, shelf or garden will do. Hang the door in a place that's special to you.
  2. Once your door is hung, we can stop by and have some fun!
  3. We love to visit children who use good manners and are kind, so be sure to keep these rules in mind
  4. Do you know what's even better? Doing nice things for others, like giving a letter. Here's a little hint for you we love to get letters and drawings, too!
  5. If we haven't visited in a while, be sure to keep your biggest smile. When you're a fairy there's lots to do, but we will never forget about you.
  6. We also love pictures of all our fun, so share some online with everyone!
  7. If you're wondering why we visit at night, it's because that's when our world is light.
