How it Works

Here are some tips from your very fairy friends
to help you make it easier for them to come and visit!

A wall, shelf or garden will do. Hang your door in a place that's special for you.
Once your door is hung, we can stop by and have some fun.
We love to visit children who use good manners and are kind, so please be sure to keep these things in mind!
Do you know what's even better? Doing nice things for others - we love it when you leave us drawings or special letters.
If we haven't come to visit in a while, make sure you keep your biggest smile. When you're a fairy there's lot's to do, but don't worry because we won't forget about you!
We want everyone to know about our fun, so share it online with everyone!
Are you wondering why we come at night? It's because that's when our world is light.
Now there's nothing left to do, except wait for us to visit you!
