A Very Fairy Door is a great parenting tool to help children.

Uses include:

  • Reward good behavior
  • Help with bedtime routines
  • Encourage creativity and kindness
  • Celebrate special occasions (i.e. birthdays, Valentine's Day, etc.)
  • Be a portal for the Tooth Fairy and holiday elves
  • Provide encouragement
  • Helps children to overcome fears (i.e. the dark, needles, etc.)
  • Facilitates imaginative play
  • Assists with difficult subjects (i.e. divorce, loss of a loved one)
  • Helps during times of illness
  • Facilitates potty training
  • Provides first day of school fun
  • Be a decoration
  • Captures childhood joy and wonder
  • Teaches children about doing good for others
  • Is always there when a friend is needed
  • Encourgages more family time and less screen time
  • Helps autistic and ADHD kids to focus
  • Most importantly, spreads love just because

The possibilities for year round FUN are endless! A Very Fairy Door is a unique gift that sparks imaginations, encourages creativity and celebrates make believe.
