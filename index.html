Welcome!

The excitement begins once the fairies move in!

"A Very Fairy Door" is a 32 page, beautifully illustrated, 8X8 hardcover children's book that comes packaged with a fairy door to be hung on a wall, shelf, or secret garden — pretty much anywhere you like.

Written for ages 3 and up — and for anyone who believes in make believing!
